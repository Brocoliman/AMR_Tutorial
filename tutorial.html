Setup for Algodoo(the app to make Marble Races!)

This setup tutorial is only supported by PCs, not mobile devices. The actual marble race making tutorial can work on mobile, though.
To start, go along with the following steps:
  1. Go to the Algodoo Download Page
  2. Scroll down to the Free Downloads and choose the download corresponding to your computer
  3. Follow the rest of instructions given by the download

Basic Tools

Go to the bottom right corner, and you will see a toolkit. Go ahead, experiment all you want!

Making Marbles

To start, go along with the following steps:
  1. Use the Circle Tool to create a circle of your desired size
  2. Right click on the circle, and click on appearance. Don't get too bewildered, and if you do, you will regret clicking on the script menu (The script menu will be the most important thing you use later on)
  3. Take a look at the checkboxes below the color picker. Make sure Borders, Protractor, and most importantly, Circle Pie are unchecked. Then, of course, pick the color of your marble
  4. These are the 15 standard marble colors for me in RGB:
    1. Red(255,0,0)
    2. Maroon(135,0,0)
    3. Orange(255,140,0)
    4. Brown(135,70,0)
    5. Yellow(255,255,0)
    6. Olive(135,135,0)
    7. Lime(0,255,0)
    8. Green(0,135,0)
    9. Cyan(0,255,255)
    10. Blue(0,0,255)
    11. Magenta(255,0,255)
    12. Purple(135,0,255)
    13. Black(0,0,0)
    14. Gray(135,135,135)
    15. White(255,255,255)
  5. Re-enter the menu, and go to the Geometry Actions. Click Add Tracer.
