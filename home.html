Intro

Of course, if you're here, then your interested in making Marble Races. Here's my thoughts about making Marble Races:
  1. You'll need to learn many contraptions by yourself, for example, I learned to make "Break the Wall" by myself, but of course I'll show you how to make the contraptions I had to make
  2. Be familiar with coding, since based on number 1, you may need to figure out many special contraptions yourself
  3. Make sure to change your series subject and switch out of the Classic Marble Race Track quick, or otherwise the audience might get bored
